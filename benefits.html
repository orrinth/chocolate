Are there benefits to eating chocolate?

The answer is yes, but that depends on the type of chocolate. Of course, there are little to no benefits when it comes to artificial, sugary chocolate. You want to make sure that you eat the more healthy, natural chocolate to gain those benefits, especially dark chocolate. It's said that the darker the chocolate, the healthier the chocolate.
